About the Project
Diamond City

Diamond City is a prestigious luxury residential development situated in the heart of Kadıköy, Istanbul's most vibrant and cosmopolitan district. With breathtaking Bosphorus views from every floor, the project redefines modern luxury living in Turkey's largest city.

Developed by Osan Development Group, Diamond City comprises 85 meticulously designed residences across 20 floors. Every apartment features floor-to-ceiling windows, premium Italian finishes, and smart home automation systems — setting a new standard for sophisticated urban living in Istanbul.
